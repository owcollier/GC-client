“The Crest” is a boutique college prep school serving 450 students in grades 6 through 12 with a curriculum focused on music, entertainment technology, and STEM. Students enroll in core, foundation, and elective courses infused with entrepreneurship and career education. Skills are strengthened by practical application and knowledge expanded in diverse learning environments.

TEACHING & LEARNING ENVIRONMENT

  • Small and large group settings
  • Class size: 15
  • Courses in the classroom, online, and in the community
  • Individual, team projects, and independent study
  • Labs and Meet-Ups
  • One-on-one support
  • Rigorous yet nurturing academic environment
  • Safe to take intellectual risks

CURRICULUM

  • 21 credits for middle school; 30 credits for high school
  • Dual Enrollment and Advanced Placement Courses
  • Instructors: certified teachers, business and music industry reps, and college professors
  • Select one of four majors:
    • Music Production & Entrepreneurship
    • Game Innovation Design & Entrepreneurship
    • Business of Communication
    • Business of Science

GRADUATION MODEL

  • Total credits reflect a high profile with universities
  • 3 graduation options:
    • 3 years with a high school diploma
    • 4 years with a high school diploma and an AA Degree
    • 5 years with a high school diploma
  • Diplomas: Superlative, Exemplary, and Distinction
  • Models exceed State of Florida requirements
